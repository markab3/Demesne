# Military

**Status:** Draft

## Unit Upkeep

Military units consume gold per tick. Units operating outside territory additionally consume authority upkeep per tick.

## The General

Projecting military force outside territory requires a General. Generals cost **Authority** upkeep, not gold — creating a meaningful constraint. A city rich in gold but Authority-starved cannot project force even if it can afford the units.

**General mechanics:**
- Loyalty ratings affected by consecutive losses, unpaid upkeep, or rival poaching
- A general can defect and take their army — creates espionage gameplay opportunity
- Each general has a command radius — armies too far from their general suffer attrition

## Siege Mechanics

Sieges take real game-time — walls must be breached over multiple in-game days.

- Established cities gain **fortification ratings** that slow siege progress
- Allies can send relief forces during an ongoing siege
- An established city should take many game-days to fall, not a single tick

## Offline Protection

No military movement or tile claiming occurs outside the activity window. Warfare is impossible against an offline player. The frozen window provides structural protection without a separate defensive mechanic.

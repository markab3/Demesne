# Education

**Status:** Draft

Books feed directly into specialist development, closing the production loop.

> Parchment + Ink → Books → Scriptorium → Literacy skill → Accounting / Alchemy / Tactics unlocks

## Literacy

Literacy is a prerequisite skill for secondary skill unlocks. A specialist without literacy can only advance through hands-on guild training, which is slower. A city with a scriptorium and steady book supply trains specialists measurably faster.

## Education Buildings

| Building | Requires | Grants |
|---|---|---|
| Scriptorium | Parchment, Ink, Literate Specialist | Produces books, trains literacy |
| Guild Hall | Relevant Master Specialist | Accelerates Craft Rating, trains apprentices |
| Apothecary School | Books + Herbalist | Unlocks advanced medicinal skills |
| Military Academy | Books + Veteran General | Trains tactics skill on officers |
| Counting House | Books + Literate Merchant | Unlocks accounting — trade efficiency bonus |
| University | Books + multiple literate specialists | Unlocks advanced skills across all trades |

## Skill Adjacency

Secondary skills unlock through education and create cross-domain value:
- A blacksmith with literacy can become a guild administrator
- A physician with alchemy training unlocks the apothecary chain
- A merchant with accounting generates better trade route yields

## Book Exports

Cities that export books to trade partners export a training speed bonus — a genuinely unique economic identity with no direct military equivalent.
